♥ MY FAVOURITE HOBBY ♥

CYCLING

I love to ride my bicycle around my housing estate and enjoy the fresh air and peaceful surroundings. Every month, I make time to spend at least one day cycling. It helps me relax and take a break from my daily routine.Cycling is also a popular choice for people who want to lose weight and stay healthy. This is because cycling is a great form of exercise that helps burn calories and improve heart health. When we cycle, our bodies sweat, which helps remove toxins and keeps our skin healthy. It also strengthens our leg muscles and improves our overall fitness.In addition, cycling is an environmentally friendly way to travel. It does not produce pollution like cars or motorcycles, and it helps reduce traffic in busy areas. For people who want to stay physically active and take care of the environment, cycling is a perfect option.






PLAYING VIDEO GAME

My second hobby is playing video games on my phone. I have always loved spending my free time exploring virtual worlds, and one of my favorite games to play is PUBG. When I play PUBG, I have to think carefully about my moves, plan my attacks, and decide the best time to defend or strike. This helps me sharpen my decision-making skills and stay focused under pressure. I enjoy the thrill of trying different strategies and improving my performance with each game. Also, it combines excitement, creativity, learning, and friendship all in one. It makes me happy, helps me relax, and gives me something to look forward to whenever I have free time.






BADMINTON

My last hobby is badminton. Badminton is a sport that makes me feel active and healthy. I usually play this sport with my sister when I have free time. Badminton is a game that can be played by two or four people. It has a low risk of injury, so people of all ages can enjoy it. It can helps improve concentration and focus, which can enhance mental clarity and reduce stress. That is why I love playing badminton every weekend when I am free. Besides,playing badminton can also helps us to bond and spend quality time together.It is not just a sport for me but also a way to connect with my loved ones. I always look forward to playing badminton every weekend when I have the chance. It has become an important and enjoyable part of my life.
